A colourful merging puzzle game!

Placing three or more same colour pieces of paint next to each other starts a chain reaction. Make these chain reactions to upgrade your paint and earn more points!

Tap your current piece to rotate it. You can also throw it away or stash it for later use.

Every point you make will leave a mark on the canvas, share your painting when the game is over!

Features:

  • Easy to learn!
  • Colourful graphics!
  • Share your painting!
