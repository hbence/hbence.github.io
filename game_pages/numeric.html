These little machines wish nothing more than to reach equilibrium. They need your help to do that!

The puzzles start out easy but it won't take long to see why they need your help. The simple additions and subtractions of the early levels will get more challenging as rails, blocks, locks and conveyor belts appear. They need your guidence through 47 increasingly difficulty levels. Usually there are multiple solutions, be creative!

Features:

  • Simple, one-touch controls
  • 47 Levels
  • Most levels have mutliple solutions
  • Challenging gameplay, gradual difficulty
  • 4 Available skins
  • Completely free!
