Connect as many circles as you can to make the longest possible chain! Close the line to make an explosion and take all the adjacent circles! There are bonus circles which will give you plus time or extra points if you can connect them.

In Blitz mode you have 60 seconds to get the most point, unless you connect some bonus time circles. In Move mode you have as much time as you need but you can only make 30 connections.

Features:

  • Easy, simple gameplay
  • One touch control
  • Two game modes
  • Game Center, Google Play
  • Completely free!
